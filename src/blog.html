Here is our services below for more information.

Our in-house shops fabricate, integrate, and manufacture some of the most reliable, and often unique, off-grid systems available. Our experienced engineers and technicians are highly skilled at component and electrical design/integration and our quality control and testing procedures ensure we deliver tough, functional, systems that perform in the field.

Additionally, we use high-quality materials to custom fabricate a range of control panels, structures, wiring, mounts and other components for our customers.

First and foremost, Ameresco Solar is a design, engineering, and solar integration company that specializes in creating custom off-grid systems. We excel in developing both small and large scale systems for industrial customers in oil and gas, railroad, telecommunications, security, water pumping and management, traffic, and other sectors.

Secondly, we serve as a solar component supplier for a network of solar retailers, installers, contractors, and industrial end users. We carry and stock a wide range of solar components in our domestic warehouse locations (TX, MD, LA, CA, and AZ). We also distribute our own line of C1D2 solar panels, battery and electrical component enclosures, mounting brackets, and pre-wired backplate assemblies.

Also We design and engineer grid-tie, grid-tie with battery backup, and residential solar power systems. We assist installers and contractors by providing a one stop solution for products, engineering services, and project management. We also offer the Ameresco Solar Financing Program for our contractors and dealers.
